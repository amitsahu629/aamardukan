About AamarDukan

Your trusted online shopping destination in India

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShopEasy started with a simple mission: to make online shopping accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone in India. What began as a small startup has grown into one of India's most trusted e-commerce platforms.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality products at fair prices, regardless of where they live. That's why we've built a platform that connects buyers with sellers across the country, offering everything from daily essentials to specialty items.

Our Mission

To revolutionize online shopping in India by providing a seamless, secure, and satisfying customer experience while supporting Indian businesses and manufacturers.

Our Vision

To become India's most customer-centric online marketplace, where people can discover and purchase anything they need with just a few clicks.

Our Values

Customer First

We put our customers at the center of everything we do, ensuring their satisfaction with every purchase.

Trust & Security

We maintain the highest standards of security and transparency to build lasting trust with our customers.
